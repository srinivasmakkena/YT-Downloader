"ffmpeg -? or ffmpeg -h" is for basic help


ffmpef -h specific topic
  	eg:
		ffmpeg -h decoder=flv

Available codecs
	ffmpeg -codecs

Available decoders
	ffmpeg -decoders

Available filters
	ffmpeg -filters

Available formats
	ffmpeg -formats
	
Available pixel formats
	ffmpeg -pix_fmts

Available protocols
	ffmpeg -protocols
